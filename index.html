About Our Studio

Founded in 2020, Sounds-of-the-Times-Studios has been at the heart of Leipzig's vibrant music scene. Our state-of-the-art facility offers world-class recording, mixing, and mastering services.

We specialize in creating the perfect acoustic environment for artists across all genres. Our team of experienced engineers is dedicated to bringing your creative vision to life with technical excellence and artistic sensitivity.

Located in a historic building in Leipzig's city center, our studio combines modern technology with classic analog warmth.

Our Services

Recording

Professional recording in our acoustically treated live rooms with premium microphones and preamps.

Mixing

Expert mixing services using both analog outboard gear and the latest digital tools.

Mastering

Final polish and preparation for distribution across all platforms and formats.

Sound Design

Custom sound effects and audio post-production for film, games, and media.
